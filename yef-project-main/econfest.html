Young Economists’ Forum is the only elite academic club of North South University, which was founded in 1998, and ever since has been extraordinarily active in promoting business and economics-related concerns. Indeed, we have various events including competitions and member development programs, one of which is the most unique and insightful research-based competition, “ECONFEST”.

In each of its brilliant chapters, Econfest has had tremendously captivating agendas which have combined business, economics, and environmental related concerns. The competition is truly acclaimed for delving deep into the World of Economics and presenting them most intriguingly. Furthermore, each time, the event has been graciously attended by distinguished economists, faculty members, and academics.

From its 2nd edition onwards, Speakonomics is the main highlight of this esteemed competition. In this round, participants are required to submit and present their written research papers on the given economic themes. The competition is made even more intriguing by bestowing prize money awards to the esteemed winners. The Experts’ Dialogue session is also prominently organized as a crucial part of the event where eminent economists hold distinguished discussions on the most important economic issues.
