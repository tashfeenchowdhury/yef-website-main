Equilibrium is the most acclaimed research-based and academic publication on concurrent social and economic challenges, orchestrated by the Young Economists’ Forum, the finest academic club at North South university. It is the rostrum for the blazing young researchers, academics, entrepreneurs and professionals YEF has produced to flaunt the eccentric knowledge and vision they have cumulated throughout their stint in the club. Equilibrium is the monolith of everything good YEF has done to date, so much so that it has cemented itself as the stalwart publication in the business education fraternity in the country as well as abroad.

The release of a publication completely by a student-administered organization did cast some doubt, but YEF has overwhelmingly nullified that by rolling out 13 successful editions of Equilibrium to meet the thirst of vivacious readers. The publication covers all the concurrent economic problems and discussions like economic growth domestically and globally, financial inclusion, policy-making challenges for particular issues, money laundering, the toll climate change is taking on economies, financial crimes, and the recommendations for all the problems identified.

Young Economists’ Forum draws its motivation from the ambition of being a flag-bearer of Bangladesh’s business education community. YEF has always pledged to add to the stature of the North South University School of Business and Economics, and Equilibrium has been the medium to realizing this achievement. Equilibrium reflects the outcome of the flagship national events, workshops and public lectures the club organizes. This truly makes the publication the crown jewel of YEF.
