As the term comes to a close and we usher in a new era for our beloved club, it is finally time to introduce the squad of elites that will be bearing the torch of leadership for YEF. They are part of the exclusive roster of individuals who have etched their names in the history of our renowned club as some of the most dedicated, passionate, and skilled members. Together, they form the pillars holding up this vibrant community and serve as inspirations for the generations to come. Young Economists' Forum proudly congratulates the Executive Body of the term 2023-24 and welcomes them wholeheartedly to take the reins and lead us into new horizons.
