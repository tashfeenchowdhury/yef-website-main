The world of business as we know it is constantly evolving, and this is amplified during the pandemic. With several enterprises and entities relocating a large portion of their operations to the virtual realm, it is clear that we have gradually but steadily arrived at a new normal. With this specific thought in mind, we recognize that during uncertain and perilous times such as these, the role of business consultancy becomes more critical than ever. Thus, in order to provide students with a platform for channeling the inner consultants in them, the North South University Young Economists' Forum (NSU YEF) presents 𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐍𝐞𝐰 𝐕𝐞𝐫𝐬𝐞 𝐨𝐟 𝐄𝐜𝐨𝐧𝐬𝐢𝐥𝐢𝐮𝐦.

With the distinction of being the country's largest and first-ever consultancy-based business competition, The New Verse of Econsilium aims to not only provide students with an opportunity to practice their craft in business consultancy by utilizing their perceptual, analytical, and discerning abilities, but also to assess their ability in making sound top-level decisions.
