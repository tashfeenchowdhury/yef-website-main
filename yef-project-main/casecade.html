In the fast-paced world of business and economics, it is not just about making the right decisions anymore. Instead, it is about the thrill of making those decisions promptly, which require intelligent thinking. Keeping this in mind, Young Economists' Forum (YEF) brings the same thrill to you with "Casecade - Quarantine Challenge Week".

CASECADE is a business competition that lets undergraduate students test their ability of strategic planning, modelling and decision making. Most importantly, it tests their ability to perform under pressure. Participants will have to brainstorm, ideate and work their way to the championship and be rewarded.
